BRouter is a highly flexible router specializing in bike routing. Osm2pgsql is
used to read OSM data into a database for extensive preprocessing to add useful
information as "pseudo tags" to the routing graph. This way routes through
quiet forest and along scenic rivers can be preferred over dangerous
intersections and busy city streets.
