Spyglass shows all tagged OpenStreetMap objects everywhere in the world, more
than a billion objects with all their details available. The data is updated
every minute. Spyglass is intended as a tool to help OSM mappers in their work,
but it is also fun to rummage around in the data just to see the width and
breath of the data available in OSM.
